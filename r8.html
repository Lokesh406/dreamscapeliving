Hollywood Glam Interior Design Style



Hollywood Glam: Also referred to as Hollywood Regency, this style is heavily influenced by the excess of the 1920s and 1930s and the movie industry during this time. Hollywood glam includes vintage items and often features Art Deco patterns with clean, geometric lines.Inspired by the golden age of cinema, a Hollywood Glam interior design style brings forth a sense of sophistication, luxury, and timeless beauty. With its plush textures, rich colors, and dazzling accents, this style captures the essence of Hollywood's allure and creates a space that exudes glamour and indulgence
